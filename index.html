Covid 19 Dashboard

In this project, we dive deep into the global impact of COVID-19 vaccination. Using reliable datasets and interactive visualisations created with JavaScript, particularly Leaflet.js, we scrutinise vaccination progress, disparities, and correlations. Our goal is to shed light on vaccination coverage, distribution, and its intricate relationship with factors such as GDP per capita. Through our findings, we underscore the critical importance of ensuring equitable vaccine access for all. Join us on this journey of discovery into the intricacies of global vaccination efforts.

Covid 19 Dashboard

Analysing House Prices In London

In this project, we used Python and Pandas to analyse property prices in various London boroughs. Our journey begins with data collection, where we meticulously identify and gather data from diverse sources, including public databases, CSV files, and APIs. Following this, we apply data extraction and cleansing techniques to ensure the data's reliability. Then, we delve into data analysis, embarking on a journey to uncover hidden patterns and trends. We created visualisations to provide a clear picture of the data and conduct statistical analyses, such as calculating mean and median, to address our research questions effectively.

Analysing House Prices In London

CitiBike

This analysis delves into the usage patterns and user demographics of Citi Bike, a bike-sharing system in New Jersey, based on the available data from 2021. By examining key insights from the dataset, we aim to gain a deeper understanding of the popular starting stations, user types, gender distribution, and trip durations by using Tableau. This analysis provides valuable information for improving the bike-sharing system's efficiency, understanding user preferences, and enhancing transportation infrastructure in the region.

CitiBike
